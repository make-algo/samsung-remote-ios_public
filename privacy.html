Privacy Policy.

TL;DR - iOS TV Remote collects no personal data. The app does not require an account, does not track you, and does not sell or share personal information. It uses your local network to find and control compatible TVs.

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how iOS TV Remote (the App, we, our) handles information when you use the application.

Information We Collect

We collect no personal information. The App does not collect, process, or transmit:

  • Your name, email address, or other identifying information
  • Location data, unless explicitly described in this policy
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics, unless explicitly described in this policy
  • Payment information
  • Personal or sensitive data

Local Storage

The App may save one saved TV record, pairing or reconnect information, and basic app settings locally on your device. This data stays on your device, is not sold, and can be removed by deleting the App or clearing app data.

Local Network Use

The App uses your local Wi-Fi network to discover, pair with, reconnect to, and send remote-control commands to compatible Samsung Smart TVs. These communications are intended to stay on your local network and are used only to provide the remote-control experience.

Third-Party Services and Libraries

The App may use the limited services or libraries listed below to provide TV discovery and command transport. Samsung TVs and local network services are controlled by their respective providers and may behave differently by model or firmware.

  • Local Samsung TV network services: Discovering, pairing with, and sending remote-control commands to compatible TVs on the same Wi-Fi network
  • TVCommanderKit: App dependency used for Samsung TV discovery, connection, and command transport

Children's Privacy

iOS TV Remote does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because no personal data is collected by the App, no children's data is sold or shared.

Changes to This Policy

If our privacy practices change, we will update this page and revise the "Last updated" date.
