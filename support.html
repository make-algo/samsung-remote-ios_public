Support & Help

Get help with Samsung Remote or contact us about technical issues, bugs, and feature requests.

Getting Started

Make sure your iPhone and compatible Samsung TV are on the same Wi-Fi network, then allow Local Network access and search for your TV.

Pairing Help

Some TVs show an approval prompt before the app can connect. Approve the request on the TV screen when it appears.

Reconnect Issues

Check that the TV is powered on, the iPhone is on the same Wi-Fi network, and Local Network permission remains enabled.

Model Compatibility

The MVP is being validated against Samsung Q70 Series and Samsung The Frame 55. Other compatible Samsung Smart TVs may work but are not guaranteed.
